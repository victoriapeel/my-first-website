A little bit about me.

I can't tell you how or where or when I discovered coding, but when I did I was hooked. I love identifying and solving 'problems', and I love it even more if the solution looks beautiful! I design and code but I'm not an artist or writer, so I work well with people who have their content ready to go. If you're not ready with prose or pictures I have trusted partners who can help you with the words and images for your site.

Whilst I work very well on my own, I also love to collaborate! I think peer-to-peer learning is invaluable and I am always looking for opportunities to work within a larger team and expand my knowledge. I'm currently based on the East coast of Canada, but I love to travel; hopping on a plane to meet you and your team would be a delight and am always open to the possibility of relocating.

Clean Designs: PSD to Code

I love to create clean simple interfaces which I can quickly & accurately take from PSD into a webpage that is pixel-perfect.

Hand-Coded HTML

My focus is writing clean, well-formatted, semantic HTML5 by hand to make sure that the content is easy to read, easy to collaborate, trouble-shoot and accessible.

Well-Organized CSS

I pride myself on writing CSS that is easy to read and build on. I focus on keeping my css lean and fast to load, and I make it a habit to stay up to date on current best practices.
